My Role Model

Swami Vivekananda

"Arise, Awake, and Stop Not Till the Goal is Reached"

About Him

Swami Vivekananda (1863–1902) was a great Indian philosopher, spiritual leader, and disciple of Sri Ramakrishna Paramahamsa. He introduced Indian philosophies of Vedanta and Yoga to the Western world and played a major role in awakening national pride among Indians.

Why He Inspires Me

  • He taught self-confidence, strength, and fearlessness.
  • He believed that education is the manifestation of perfection already in man.
  • He emphasized service to humanity as service to God.
  • He inspired youth to build character and lead a meaningful life.

Key Contributions

  • Represented India at the Parliament of the World’s Religions (Chicago, 1893).
  • Founded the Ramakrishna Mission to promote education and social service.
  • Revived Indian spiritual heritage and national consciousness.
  • His teachings continue to inspire leaders, students, and thinkers worldwide.

Inspirational Quote

“You cannot believe in God until you believe in yourself.”

Legacy

Swami Vivekananda’s life is a powerful reminder that inner strength, discipline, and service to society are the true foundations of success. His vision of a strong, educated, and confident India continues to guide generations even today.
